In the world of programming, legends like "Tourist" and the grandmasters of competitive coding inspire awe. Whether you're a beginner or striving to be the #1 in the world, this handbook is your step-by-step guide to mastering Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) and Competitive Programming (CP).
